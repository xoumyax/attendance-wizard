üìù Marking Attendance

3 Get the Token from Your Instructor

Your instructor will generate a unique 6-digit token during class:

  • The instructor will display or announce the token
  • The token is ONLY valid for 5 minutes (regular classes)
  • Test sessions have 24-hour validity for practice
  • Write down or remember the 6-digit code
‚è∞ Time Restriction: Regular attendance can only be marked between 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM. Make sure you're in class on time!
üí° Example Token: 123456 (6 digits)
4 Mark Your Attendance

Once you have the token:

  • You'll automatically see today's sessions after login
  • Select the current class session
  • Enter the 6-digit token provided by instructor
  • Click "Mark Attendance"
  • You'll see a success message if recorded correctly
‚úÖ Success! Once marked, you cannot mark the same session twice. Your attendance is permanently recorded.
‚ö†Ô∏è Cannot Mark Attendance? Check:
  • Is it between 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM?
  • Is the token correct? (no spaces, 6 digits)
  • Is the token still valid? (not expired)
  • Have you already marked this session?
5 View Your Records

Check your attendance history anytime:

  • After logging in, scroll down to see your statistics
  • View Total Sessions (all classes scheduled)
  • View Attended Sessions (classes you've marked)
  • See your Attendance Percentage
  • Check your Grade Points (out of 10)
üìä Grading Scale:
  • 10 points: ‚â•85% attendance
  • 8 points: 75-84% attendance
  • 5 points: 50-74% attendance
  • 0 points: <50% attendance
